## About Me

Solutions Architect at Strategic Communications, supporting federal and SLED clients across cloud procurement, migration, and modernization through a 170+ partner ecosystem. I lead product development for Justice and Public Safety clients - architecting CJIS-compliant AWS GovCloud solutions for law enforcement agencies - and drive agentic engineering adoption across the organization.

Alongside that I run [Nobler Works](https://noblerworks.com/), which my cofounder and I bootstrapped to profitability within 3 months - no investors, no patrons, no sponsors - and have since shipped 10+ products across SaaS, B2B growth tools, and open-source intelligence.

Before this I spent 3.5 years as a Solutions Architect at AWS (two Quarterly All-Star awards, top 1% of field) working with public sector, federal partners, and the Intelligence Community, where I drove early RAG enablement on SageMaker/OpenSearch, led Bedrock and SageMaker workshops, and co-published two AWS technical blogs. I hold 18 multi-cloud certifications across AWS, GCP, and Azure - part of 60 total certifications and accreditations - with hands-on experience building production RAG and agentic systems, designing scalable infrastructure, and leading technical engagements from scoping through delivery.
